Privacy Policy

This mailing list is managed by Team Moving Still. You can contact us at mailing-list@moving0still.com.

What we collect:

We collect your email address when you sign up via the form on our website or opt in through platforms like Resident Advisor, where you have explicitly agreed to receive marketing communications from Moving Still.

How we use your data:

Your email address is used to send occasional updates, gig news, music drops, and perks related to Moving Still. We do not share your data with third parties.

Legal basis:

We process your data based on your consent.

Where your data is stored:

We use Google Forms and Google Sheets to collect and manage email addresses. We use Brevo to store subscriber data and send emails. Both platforms are GDPR-compliant and handle data securely.

How to unsubscribe:

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email or by contacting us directly at mailing-list@moving0still.com.

This policy aligns with the requirements of the Irish Data Protection Commission for electronic and direct marketing.
